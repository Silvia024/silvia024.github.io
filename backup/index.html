Silvia Rossi, PhD

Postdoctoral Researcher at CWI (Amsterdam)
Immersive Multimedia Systems & XR
ACM SIGMM Director of Conferences

I am a Senior Scientist in the Distributed and Interactive Systems (DIS) group at the Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI) in Amsterdam.

My research investigates user behaviour and system design for immersive multimedia systems, particularly social Extended Reality (XR), with the goal of enabling scalable and engaging interactive experiences.
