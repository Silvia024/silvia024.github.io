Immersive Communications

Immersive communication systems allow remote participants to experience a shared environment with six degrees of freedom (6DoF). My research in this area focuses on understanding the media requirements, network constraints and user quality of experience when transmitting volumetric content. I explore capture, coding and streaming techniques to enable interactive telepresence in AR/VR.
