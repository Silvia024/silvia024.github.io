User Behaviour Analysis in VR

This research strand focuses on understanding how users interact with immersive environments. By analysing gaze direction, head movements and physiological responses, we gain insights into attentional patterns and design adaptive media experiences. We develop tools for collecting and modelling behavioural data and evaluate how factors such as field‑of‑view, latency and scene content affect user engagement.
